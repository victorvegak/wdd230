Managua Chamber Of Commerce

This name represents the city where the Chamber operates and emphasizes its purpose.

Site Purpose

Managua Chamber of Commerce aims to:

  • Promote local businesses through member services
  • Foster economic and community development
  • Advocates for public policies that balance economic prosperity

Scenarios

  • How can I become a member of the chamber and what benefits does membership offer?
  • Is there a calendar of upcoming local business events, and how can I participate?
  • Where can I access resources and guides for starting a new business in the area?

Color Schema

Our selected color scheme includes:

  • Primary Color(#336699) used for headings
  • Secondary Color (#99CC99) - used for background

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font: 'Robot', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
